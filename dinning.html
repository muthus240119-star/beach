DINNING

Multi-Cuisine Beachfront Restaurant

• Our signature restaurant serves a wide variety of dishes including traditional South Indian specialties, North Indian favorites, Continental classics, and fresh seafood delights. Guests can enjoy freshly caught fish, prawns, and crab prepared with authentic coastal spices. Every dish is crafted using high-quality ingredients to ensure rich flavor and perfect presentation.

Romantic Candlelight Beach Dining

• For couples and honeymoon guests, we offer exclusive candlelight dinners right by the shore. Soft music, gentle sea breeze, and beautifully arranged tables under the stars create a magical dining experience. Customized menus and private service make every evening special and memorable.

Poolside Café & Refreshment Bar

• Our poolside café serves light snacks, mocktails, fresh juices, and desserts throughout the day. Guests can relax with a refreshing drink while enjoying the pool view. From crispy starters to delicious pastries, the café is perfect for casual dining and evening relaxation.

Live BBQ & Seafood Nights

• On weekends, we host live barbecue and seafood grill nights. Guests can watch our chefs prepare freshly grilled dishes right in front of them. The lively ambiance with music and beach vibes makes it a favorite attraction for families and groups.

Hygienic & Comfortable Dining Environment

• We maintain the highest standards of cleanliness and hygiene. Our dining areas are spacious, well-ventilated, and designed to provide comfort with elegant décor inspired by the sea and sand.
