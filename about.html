About Us

• Welcome to Salt & Sand Beach Resort, where the soothing rhythm of the waves meets comfort, elegance, and warm hospitality. Located along a beautiful coastline, our resort is designed to offer guests a perfect blend of relaxation and luxury.


• At Salt & Sand, we believe every stay should feel special. Our spacious and well-furnished rooms provide stunning sea views, modern amenities, and a peaceful atmosphere that helps you unwind from busy city life. Whether you are planning a romantic getaway, a family vacation, or a weekend retreat with friends, our resort offers the ideal setting.


Guests can enjoy:

🌊 Direct beach access with breathtaking sunrise and sunset views
🏊 A refreshing swimming pool with poolside lounge areas
🍽️ Multi-cuisine restaurant serving fresh and delicious dishes
🎉 Event and celebration spaces for weddings, parties, and corporate gatherings
🛎️ Friendly staff dedicated to providing exceptional service
